About Me:
Currently, I am affliated to Synopsys, Bangalore as a Senior R & D Engineer in the Design Group. Earlier, I worked in BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus as an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Computer Science. Before that I was Dual Degree (M.tech+Ph.D) student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati (IIT Guwahati). I did my undergraduate, B.Tech, in Electronics and Communication Engineering. My research specializations are in Computer Architecture. Specifically, my current research interest is in Chip Multiprocessors, NOC (Network on Chip), Self-Adaptive System, and Memory (3D-stacked) organization in Chip-Multiprocessor system.

Research Interests:
Computer Architecture, Chip-Multiprocessor, Emerging Technologies such as 3D-stacked Memories, Optical Network-on-Chip etc.
